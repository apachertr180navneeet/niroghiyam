Purpose of the Privacy Policy
Niroghyam Alphadata LLP is committed to the protection of privacy of data and will take all reasonable steps to protect the personal data belonging to users of the App. This privacy policy outlines how the App collects, processes and uses personal data of individuals in compliance with the ABDM Health Data Management Policy (“HDM Policy”), available at Health Data Management Policy and the provisions of other applicable laws. ABDM understands and respects user’s privacy and it is committed to protect it. Any collection of personal or sensitive personal data under ABDM may be done only with the consent of the data principal.
Collection, Use and Storage Limitation
App shall ensure that

    1. collection and use of personal data from user is done only to the extent necessary for the purposes of processing;
    2. the processing of all personal data will be done in a fair and reasonable manner, ensuring the privacy of the user;
    3. No personal data shall be transferred by the App without the consent of the user;
    4. the personal data collected will not be retained beyond the period necessary to satisfy the purpose for which it is collected.

The personal data mentioned above in (B) may be collected using the following methods or mechanisms:

  1. Following are the methods or mechanisms for the collection of personal data:
    1. User provides the required personal details using self-registration on the App using Mobile OTP Authentication. In this mode of registration, user self declares the requested fields mentioned above; or
    2. User provides the required personal details using self-registration on App using email OTP Authentication. In this mode of registration, user self declares the requested fields mentioned above; or
    3. Lastly if the ABHA (number) of the user has been already created then the user can fetch the required personal details from the ABHA system itself using mobile OTP or Aadhar OTP authentication.
  2. Personal data may also be collected incidentally by Niroghyam Alphadata in the following instances, where the user:
    1. Provides the App with personal data such as name, date of birth, email id, phone number
    2. Completes an online suggestions, complaints or grievance redressal form by registering on the App at https://niroghyam.com, or contacts us or emails us at info@niroghyam.com
